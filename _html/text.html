Why is Credits different from a crypto exchange?

Credit Wallet offers payment options similar to those in an ordinary bank.
Credits allows you to pay and receive money from anyone, without any limitations on whom you can pay and receive from.
However, on crypto exchanges, users can only deposit and withdraw money from customer accounts; they can't send money to friends and family or receive a salary.
On Credits, users can do it all!
